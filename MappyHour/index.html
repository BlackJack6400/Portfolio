Mappy Hour!

Mappy Hour is a mobile application created to help adults hoping to save money when going out to
eat or drink to solve the issue of finding affordable restaurants and bars.

We find you happy hour deals for happy hour times!

The Problem

We believe that special deals like happy hour for food and drinks will make outings more enticing and make gathering more enjoyable for people in their social experiences, and that we might be able to help by creating an app that can locate special deals and happy hour details nearby.
