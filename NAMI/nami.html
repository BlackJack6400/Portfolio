National Alliance of Mental Illness (NAMI OC)

NAMI OC is a non-profit organization that is dedicated to support Orange County residents and families who live with mental disabilities.

The Problemn

We believe that their public outreach is partially limited from a lack of a dynamic website in a digital era.

The Solution

By making NAMI OC’s website more intuitive and highlighting their educational programs and events, visitors can be motivated to participate while the organization expands its productivity and reach within the county.
