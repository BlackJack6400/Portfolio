NAMI OC Redesign

NAMI OC is a non-profit organization that is dedicated to positively impacting Orange County families who deal with mental disabilities. They are dedicated to promoting events such as walks to raise money and awareness of mental illnesses. We believe that their public image suffers from a lack of a dynamic website in a digital era. By re-designing their website to highlight their support programs and motivate visitors to donate or participate in their fundraising events, the organization can increase its productivity and extend their reach within the county.
