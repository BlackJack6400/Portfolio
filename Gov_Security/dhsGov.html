Department of Homeland Security Re-Design

The United States Department of Homeland Security (DHS) is a federal agency designed to protect the United States against threats. Its wide-ranging duties include aviation security, border control, emergency response and cybersecurity.

The Problemn

The DHS website is extremely content heavy. There is too much content being displayed, and users have difficulty locating the informaiton they are looking for.

The Solution

Creating a redesign with breaks for the users' eyes will help alleviate the heavy feeling and reduce the overwhelming amount of text. The navigation was cut down to make information easier to find and less of a hassel.

Future Development & ROI

  • Perform more user testing on final version
  • Redesign more pages of the site to have more user options during testing
