Summary
The Problem: Plant owners can experience roadblocks when figuring out the best way to keep their plants healthy and happy. Whether they have a new plant they are unfamiliar with, or they're new to the world of plant ownership, finding information on your plant can be difficult to find.

Our Solution: SOLi was designed to help plant owners ranging from beginner to advanced maintain healthy, thriving houseplants by providing reminders, advice, and care direction through a user-friendly mobile app.

Role: UX Researcher, UI Designer
Tools: Adobe XD, InVision, Google Drive, Trello
Time: 3 weeks
Research Data
Before starting our research, my partner and I believed users who typically forgot to take care of their plants would want notifications. However, after conducting user interviews and collecting survey data, we discovered users who remembered to take care of their plants wanted notifications and those who forgot to take care of their plants did not want notifications.

Survey Data:
52% - Never forget/Don’t usually forget to water
56% - Definitely want/Prefer notifications

Interview data:
Users want notifications as a backup
Those who did not want notifications said “If I forget, I forget”

Competitor Analysis
After researching our competitors, we found users still wanted...

  • The ability to adjust care based on climate or weather
  • A way to take notes on each individual plant’s health
  • Usability for plant owners of every experience level
  • Browse plants by category filters
  • Ideas and general tips from experienced plant owners
User Flow
  • New user onboarding involves learning about the user to present them the best experience
  • Lots of ways to learn about plants including browse and discover pages
