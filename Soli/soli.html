Summary
The Problem: We observed that while there are multiple plant-care apps on the market, the services are not meeting all the goals to help plant owners. This is causing more difficulty in users’ ability to maintain plants long-term.

Our Solution: SOLi was designed to help plant owners ranging from beginner to advanced experience maintain healthy, thriving houseplants by providing reminders, advice, and care direction through a user-friendly mobile app.

Role: UX Researcher, UI Designer
Tools: Adobe XD, InVision, Google Drive, Trello
Time: 3 weeks
